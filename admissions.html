Admissions

Welcome to the University of Sharjah's admissions page. We are excited to help you start your journey with us. Here you can find detailed information about the admissions process, requirements, and deadlines.

To apply to the University of Sharjah, please follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Review our programs and choose the one that best fits your academic and career goals.
  • Step 2: Prepare your application documents, including transcripts, recommendation letters, and a personal statement.
  • Step 3: Complete the online application form available on our website.
  • Step 4: Submit your application along with all required documents before the application deadline.
  • Step 5: Check your email regularly for updates on your application status and further instructions.

If you have any questions about the admissions process, please do not hesitate to contact our admissions office. We are here to assist you every step of the way.
