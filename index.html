About Us

The University of Sharjah is a leading institution dedicated to academic excellence, research, and community service. Our mission is to provide a high-quality education that prepares students for successful careers and lifelong learning.

With a diverse range of programs and state-of-the-art facilities, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders and innovators.

Our Programs

  • Engineering

    Explore our cutting-edge engineering programs designed to equip you with the skills needed to excel in the industry.

  • Business

    Our business programs offer a comprehensive education in management, finance, and entrepreneurship.

  • Health Sciences

    Prepare for a career in the health sector with our specialized programs in medicine, pharmacy, and allied health sciences.

  • Humanities

    Our humanities programs provide a deep understanding of cultures, languages, and social sciences.
