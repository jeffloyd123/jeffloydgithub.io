About Us

The University of Sharjah is a comprehensive academic institution that offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs. Established in 1997, our university is committed to providing high-quality education and fostering research, innovation, and community engagement.

Our mission is to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and leadership in our students while contributing to the advancement of knowledge and the betterment of society. We are proud of our diverse and inclusive community, which brings together students, faculty, and staff from various cultural and academic backgrounds.

At the University of Sharjah, we strive to create an inspiring and supportive environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and personally. We are dedicated to preparing our graduates to become global citizens and leaders in their respective fields.
